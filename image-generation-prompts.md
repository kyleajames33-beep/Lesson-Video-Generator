On the right pan, a solid brass weight labeled by mass in grams. The scale is perfectly balanced. Clean lines, minimal detail, metaphorical not literal. Teal particles, amber weight. White background.

### marginalia — "The Bridge"
> [STYLE PREFIX] A simple suspension bridge connecting two cliffs. Left cliff has a sign with a stack of counted spheres (particles/moles). Right cliff has a sign with a weighing scale (grams). The bridge deck glows subtly amber. Clean flat vector, minimal detail, metaphorical illustration. White background.

### concept — "Periodic Table Strip"
> [STYLE PREFIX] A horizontal strip showing three element squares from a periodic table: Hydrogen (H, 1.008), Carbon (C, 12.01), Oxygen (O, 16.00). Each square is a clean rounded rectangle with the element symbol centered and atomic mass below. Teal border, amber atomic numbers. Clean flat vector, white background, no additional text.

### definition — "Units Cube"
> [STYLE PREFIX] Three stacked cubes or blocks, each containing a single symbol: lowercase "m" on the top cube, lowercase "n" on the middle cube, capital "M" on the bottom cube. The blocks are slightly offset to show depth. Teal and amber accents. Clean flat vector, white background, no other text.

### lab-footage — "Lab Balance Scene"
> [STYLE PREFIX] A top-down view of a digital analytical balance on a clean white lab bench. On the balance sits a small glass beaker containing white powder. The balance display shows a numeric readout. Clean flat vector, minimal detail, no brand names, teal and amber accent colors only on the display and beaker outline. White background.

### formula — "Equation Particles"
> [STYLE PREFIX] A simple equation layout using geometric shapes instead of text. A rectangle labeled with a mass icon (small scale) on the left, an equals sign in the center, then a stack of counted spheres (moles) multiplied by a bar chart (molar mass). Clean flat vector, white background, minimal detail, teal and amber accents.

### worked-example — "CO₂ Molecule Builder"
> [STYLE PREFIX] A molecular model of carbon dioxide (CO₂) shown as a central black sphere connected to two red spheres by straight lines. Each sphere has a subtle atomic mass number floating near it: 12.01 near the central sphere, 16.00 near each outer sphere. Clean flat vector, bond angles at 180 degrees, white background, minimal detail, teal and amber accent colors for the numbers.

### misconception — "Two Scales Comparison"
> [STYLE PREFIX] Two balance scales side by side. Left scale: small fixed weight labeled "per 1 mole" — unchanging, solid, locked. Right scale: variable pile of powder on a weighing pan — larger, growing, flexible. A dashed red circle with a cross through the left scale saying "this never changes." Clean flat vector, white background, teal for fixed elements, amber for variable elements.

### quick-check — "Water Molecule with Masses"
> [STYLE PREFIX] A water molecule (H₂O) shown as a central large red sphere bonded to two smaller white spheres at a 104-degree angle. Next to each sphere floats its atomic mass: 1.008 near each hydrogen, 16.00 near the oxygen. Clean flat vector, white background, minimal detail, teal and amber accent colors for the mass numbers.

### summary — "Key Ideas Badge Set"
> [STYLE PREFIX] Five clean circular or rounded-square badges arranged in a gentle arc or vertical stack. Each badge contains a simple icon: (1) a single sphere with "1" inside, (2) a periodic table square, (3) a multiplication symbol with shapes, (4) a capital M and lowercase m separated by a not-equal sign, (5) a checkmark with unit labels. Clean flat vector, white background, teal and amber accents only.

## Export Notes for Codex / Image Generator

**When generating batches:**
1. Always prepend the **Universal Style Prefix** to every prompt.
2. Export images as **PNG with transparent background** where possible.
3. Use **square or 16:9 aspect ratios** depending on intended slide placement.
4. Avoid adding text/labels inside the images — Remotion will overlay text programmatically.
5. Keep file sizes under 2MB for fast Remotion bundling.
6. Name files descriptively: `scene-type-concept-name.png` (e.g., `hook-balance-moles-grams.png`).

**Color palette reference:**
- Background: `#ffffff` (white)
- Primary accent: `#0d6b52` (teal)
- Secondary accent: `#f0a830` (amber)
- Text/ink: `#1a1a1a` (near-black)
